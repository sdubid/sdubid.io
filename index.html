ABOUT

Early age
Vadim Dmitrijev is a young Latvian jazz trombonist and composer born into a family of musicians. Vadim started to play the piano at the age of five and began learning trombone at the age of nine. He studied at the prestigious Emils Darzins Music School and obtained a bachelor's degree in jazz music at the Jazeps Vitols Music Academy.
"I stole everything I ever heard, but mostly I stole from the horns”
– Ella Fitzgerald
Career
While studying in the last year of the Jazeps Vitols Latvian Academy of Music, Vadims was already known for his trombone improvisation skills in the local music scene. He has played with well-known groups in Latvia, such as "Very Cool People", as well as his own quartet and trio, gained a certain reputation in Hamburg, where he went to study as part of an exchange program, as well as performed in the USA with the Latvian National Guard Orchestra, participating in the "Great Plains" jazz festival in Omaha.
Now
As of October 2020 Vadim has been a part of the Latvian Radio Big Band, at the time being the youngest professional jazz trombone player in the local music scene.

In July 2021 Vadim is releasing his official debut as a songwriter and producer, presenting his debut album. The album “Chapter I” consists of 8 of his own original compositions. Each of which vary in different styles and genres of music, but are connected with a deep philosophical thought throughout the album.

ABOUT ALBUM
Chapter I is a set of 8 different musical pieces describing growth both mentally and physically during the first chapter of author’s life. Each song is a dedication to different emotions,people,places and ideas that combined together create an image of one’s personality. The author truly believes his music to be philosophical and hopes it would help the listener to dive into the memories of their own first chapters and steps in their grown up lives. Chapter I is a story of how the author became the person he is and what inspired him to make certain decisions in bis life.

PROJECTS
Trio

Vadim Dmitrijev trio is a very unique music group consisting of 3 simular thinking individuals-Jānis Pastars on guitar, Toms Kursītis on double bass and Vadim himself on trombone. The group is unique because of its sound and instrumentation. The group mostly plays jazz music blended with some original sounds and songs. Because of the “no drums” setup the trio feels refreshing. Each musician bringing their musical virtuosity and blending it together with each other to get a truly original sound will make the listeners feel relaxed and excited at the same time. This trio would perfectly fit for your day time event. Afternoon concerts, brunches,birthdays and weddings, we got you covered! Feel free to give us a call or send a message to get more information about possible dates and bookings.

Chapter I

Chapter I project is a very personal musical experience. This is the first band ever which Vadim has trusted to play his own compositions. It is put together out of most talented young musicians and mentors from Latvia. Musicians like Tuomo Uusitalo,who brings his huge experience of playing jazz and teaching,also working professionaly in New-York, to inspire young artists and to make Vadim’s music sound more appropriate for casual jazz listening fans. Or Jānis Pastars,whos guitar brilliance makes this music so colorful.Jānis is heard best on the 2nd track where he plays a killing solo!!! Toms Kursītis & Pauls Ķierpe were a part of Vadim’s first jazz quartet ever providing a very strong fundament for each song played together. Most definitely they are one of the coolest people to work together with!

FlipFlap

FlipFlap and FlipFlap brass is an international band formed in Hamburg. Originaly the idea was to form a brass section trio together with Bjorn Attle Anfinsen on trumpet and Kamil Husyainov on tenor sax.But the idea was so good other musicians wanted to join as well.So the group of very fine musicians was formed. The band is based in Hamburg where Vadim plans to spend a couple of years in the future and plays mostly original music written by it’s members.
